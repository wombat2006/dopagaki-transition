## 11.2 ドパガキは理想被験者

- Users are neither customers nor products
- The tradable asset is behavioral predictability
- Platform capitalism extracts behavioral data as raw material
- Dopagaki represents an ideal actor within attention capitalism

Industrial capitalism extracted value from labor.
Attention capitalism extracts value from behavioral signals and prediction markets.
Dopagaki is not a failure of self-control but an adaptation to the incentive structure of the attention economy.
