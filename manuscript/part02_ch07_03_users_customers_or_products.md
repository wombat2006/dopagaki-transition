## Are Users Customers or Products?

Chapter 7.3 accepted after review.

Key concepts:
- Users are neither customers nor products.
- The tradable asset is behavioral predictability.
- Platform capitalism extracts behavioral data as raw material.
- Users function as Participatory Resources.
- Dopagaki represents an ideal actor within attention capitalism.

Core thesis:
Industrial capitalism extracted value from labor. Attention capitalism extracts value from behavioral signals and prediction markets. Dopagaki is not a failure of self-control but an adaptation to the incentive structure of the attention economy.
